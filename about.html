About Me

  • I'm really someone who tries to experiment to see if I can do things a better way. I think it's rare that someone would use Vim with a dvorak keyboard layout, and it was difficult at first, but I've gotten the hang of it. A standard QWERTY user would use H, J, K, and L to navigate a text file, whereas I have to use J, C, V, and P. It's unintuitive, but it works.

I play a lot of rogue-lites, like Spelunky and Dead Cells, and I play healers in Overwatch. I've also spent too much time making Steam Controller configurations.
